Why Do We Do It

We believe that the future of construction lies in the smart integration of sustainability and digital technology. At Dimbrox, we're driven by the opportunity to make the built environment more efficient, resilient, and responsible—through innovation, not just intention. Combining our backgrounds in engineering, architecture, and energy research, we aim to empower the AEC industry with tools and insights that make better buildings possible.

Who We Are

Dimbrox is a family-founded consultancy at the crossroads of architecture, engineering, and digital innovation. With deep roots in the AEC industry and a shared passion for sustainable design, we combine hands-on experience with cutting-edge tools to help clients build smarter, greener, and more efficient structures.

Milan Rashevski is an architect and energy consultant with over a decade of international experience in sustainable design, energy efficiency, and building performance simulation. His work bridges cutting-edge research with real-world applications—ranging from dynamic energy modeling and renewable energy systems to zero-energy building strategies. Milan’s academic background, including a PhD in mathematical modeling and extensive collaboration in EU R&D projects, adds a strong scientific foundation to every project he undertakes.

At Dimbrox, Milan brings his deep expertise in low-carbon architecture and energy-smart building technologies to support clients in designing the future of sustainable construction.
